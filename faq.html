How To Use The Portal

How do I access my profile?

Go to the Homepage and enter your employee ID number (last 4 digits of your phone number).

I can't access the Portal!

Let Pablo know and we'll figure out why.

How do I add or change my profile picture?

You can simply set a profile picture on the Slack App, then let Pablo know.

What are the points in the scoreboard?

Points are a way to showcase Team Members. Points are awarded when an achievement is completed.

What are the different types of achievements?

There are 3 types of achievements:

Training: this type have tiers that can be expanded as you progress. Each tier can only be completed once.

Feat: this type can only be completed once, they serve as special milestones.

Stackable: this type can be achieved multiple times. Each time it is completed, it will reflect the number of times it has.

How do I see what I can get from completing an achievement?

Simply click on the achievement for a dialog box to pop up.

How do I unlock achievements?

Complete the requirements for the achievement, then let a Trainer know.

What is a title?

A title is a cosmetic name you can display over your name in the Team Member scoreboard.

How do I select a title?

Once you unlock a title, let a Trainer or Manager know that you'd like to use it. You may request a title change once a month.

Your selected title will display next to your name on the scoreboard.

Visit the shop and find the item you want, then let a Trainer know.
